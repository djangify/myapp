1. Overview

Welcome to the Djangify Support Policy. This document explains our payment processing system, the relationship between Djangify and our associated domains, and how to get support for products or services purchased from any Djangify-affiliated website.

2. Payment Processing

When you purchase products or services from any of our affiliated websites, the payment will be processed through Djangify:

  • The name "Djangify" will appear on your credit card statement or payment receipt
  • All payments are securely processed through Stripe
  • Receipts and invoices will be issued under the Djangify business name
  • This is normal and indicates that your payment was processed successfully

If you have questions about a charge from Djangify on your statement, please check if you've purchased from any of our associated domains listed below.

3. Associated Domains

The following websites and domains operate under the Djangify umbrella:

  • Djangify.com - Our main domain
  • Pen And I Publishing - penandipublishing.co.uk

You can verify if a website is affiliated with Djangify by contacting us directly at djangify @djangify.com.

4. Support Options

For the fastest and most effective support:

  • First, check the documentation specific to the website you purchased from
  • Each affiliated website has its own dedicated support resources
  • Use the contact form (best option) on the specific website where you made your purchase
  • Email us directly djangify@djangify.com for any support requests

Our goal is to respond to all support inquiries within 48 hours during business days.

Contact Information

For general inquiries or verification of Djangify-affiliated websites:

  • Email: djangify@djangify.com

For site-specific support, please use the contact information provided on the respective website.
